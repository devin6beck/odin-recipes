Pancakes

Description

Yum yum pancakes that are made from scratch. Takes less than 30 minutes and is better for you than what can come in a box. This recipe makes 4 servings but can be added to if you need more.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • maple syrup or honey to serve

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mash the banana with a fork.
  2. Add eggs and cinnamon. Mix until combined.
  3. Heat a skillet over medium heat. Add a spoonful of batter and cook for 3-4 minutes, then flip and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes.
  4. Serve with maple syrup or honey.
